# Retail Profit Intelligence — Enterprise Analytics Project

## Project Overview

This project performs enterprise-level retail sales and profit analysis using Python, Pandas, and data visualization tools.

The objective is to simulate real-world business intelligence workflows used in large enterprises.

---

## Business Objectives

• Identify top and worst performing regions  
• Analyze profit margin trends  
• Detect high and low performing product categories  
• Generate executive-level business summary  
• Perform enterprise feature engineering  

---

## Tools Used

• Python  
• Pandas  
• NumPy  
• Matplotlib  
• Seaborn  
• Jupyter Notebook  

---

## Enterprise Features Implemented

• Data cleaning and validation  
• Feature engineering (Profit Margin %, Year, Month, Day)  
• Sales and profit trend analysis  
• Regional and category performance analysis  
• Executive business summary generation  

---

## Key Business Insights

• Total Sales: $2.29 Million  
• Total Profit: $286,397  
• Average Profit Margin: 12.03%  

Best Region: West  
Worst Region: Central  

Best Category: Technology  
Worst Category: Furniture
